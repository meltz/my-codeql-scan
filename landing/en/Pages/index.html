Highlights

At a Glance

Singapore Pools aims to be a world-class socially responsible gaming company trusted by customers and valued by the community. Set up to provide a legal, safe and trusted avenue for people to place bets, Singapore Pools play a vital role in countering illegal gambling activities. Over the years, we have evolved rapidly to keep our games relevant while creating a secure and responsible gaming environment for all.

Each year, 97% of Singapore Pool's revenue is dedicated to the betterment of Singaporeans and the broader community. Since 2010, we have been channeling more than $2 billion to the Government in the form of taxes and duties and funding to Tote Board every year in support of good causes. Beyond that, about $5 million per year on average is in support of charities and community initiatives.
