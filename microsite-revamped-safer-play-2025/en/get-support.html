Safer Play Tips

Responsible gaming is knowing that gambling is just for fun and a form of entertainment. Stay safe with these tips!

Set a limit in advance and keep to it

Only gamble what you can afford to lose

DO NOT borrow to bet

Never chase your losses

Take a break between your play

DO NOT gamble if you do not know the rules

Balance gambling with other leisure activities

DO NOT gamble when you are depressed or upset
