Safer Play

Singapore Pools is committed to be a world-class responsible gaming company. Our professional conduct and business behaviours must be upheld to the very highest standards, ensuring a fair and safe gambling experience to protect our customers.

We care about our customers, so we will proactively remind them to play responsibly, and how to get help should they ever need it. The education of our customers to make informed decisions is the cornerstone of Responsible Gaming.

Safer Play Environment

Singapore Pools has been a World Lottery Association Level 4 Responsible Gaming Framework certified operator since 2012, the highest global level of accreditation. We enable a safe and responsible environment with customer care measures in place across both our retail and online channels.

No Betting By Persons Under 18

It is illegal for any person under the age of 18 to participate in any form of gambling at our outlets, including prize claims. ID verification may be required. To enter our Livewire venues, customers must be 21 and above.

No Betting on Credit

Payment via credit cards or credit facilities for bet placements are not accepted across both our retail and online channels.

Safer Play Reminders

Safer Play messages and reminders are prominently displayed in all our retail outlets and online channels, and the Play Responsibly logo appears in all our communication materials, including our bet slips and tickets.

Product Design and Communications

Our product design and communications strategy put Safer Play principles first. We design our games in a manner that will not be likely to encourage excessive or irresponsible play.

Responsible Game Design

  • No product designed to attract persons under the age of 18

  • Low minimum bet amount

  • Modest jackpot sizes relative to international standards, e.g. the Cascade Draw feature to ensure that if there is no winner by the fourth draw, the jackpot will go to the next winning prize level

  • Conservative range of sports and horse racing bet types available for betting

Responsible Communications

  • Do not play up the rewards of gambling to avoid encouraging addiction

  • Carry only factual and essential information in our mass media notices

  • Adhere to guidelines on the use of words, tone, imagery, and style

  • Communication materials are largely confined within our retail outlets and online channels

Care for Winners Programme

Our Care for Winners Programme is launched in partnership with the Monetary Authority of Singapore's (MAS) MoneySENSE, the national financial education initiative.

Customers who claim prizes of S$5,000 and above are provided with information on financial planning tips and Safer Play messages that advise them to:

  • Be debt-free and pay off any outstanding loans, especially the ones that incur higher interest charges, first

  • Draw up a budget for expenses, avoid drastic lifestyle changes, and think twice before spending excessively

  • Identify and fulfil financial goals such as plans for post-retirement income or children's educational expenses

  • Invest wisely by understanding the features, costs, limitations and potential risks involved for different types of savings plans and investment instruments

  • Consider sharing the joy by helping those in need

We aim to provide our winners with a positive winning experience. In line with our duty of care towards customers, we wish to help prize winners become winners in life through good management of their prize money.
